About Us

We are FRC team 7130 Futureshock, a team from Mingdao High School in Taichung, Taiwan. Established in 2017, Futureshock has achieved great results in various practice and regional competitions as a rookie team. Our members are passionate about robotics and computer engineering, and we strive to do our best in every aspect of team operations. We look forward to showcasing ourselves and Taiwan to the world.





FRC 7130 is separated into 3 groups



Institution Team is the team that design , manufacture and build the whole robot . After finishing the robot is built , power distribution is the last process before everything is complete , we also arrange the course for every season .
